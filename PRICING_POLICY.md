# SafeCadence Pricing Commitments

**Last updated:** 2026-05-25

The promises buyers can screenshot and put in their procurement file.

---

## Price stability commitments

- **No surprise price increases.** We will not raise prices on existing
  customers without **90 days' written notice** to the billing contact
  on file.
- **Annual prepay locks the rate for 12 months.** If you prepay annually,
  the per-month rate is fixed for the duration of that term, regardless
  of any list-price changes during the year.
- **Grandfathering.** When new pricing tiers launch (e.g. a new "Team"
  tier between Starter and Business), existing customers stay on their
  current plan at their current rate. No forced migration to new tiers.

---

## Refund commitments

- **30-day money-back guarantee** on the first payment for any paid
  tier. Refund processed within 5 business days, no questions asked.
- **Pro-rated refunds** for annual prepays canceled mid-term: you get
  back the unused months minus a 10% administrative deduction.
- **Failed delivery refunds:** if a paid service we're contractually
  obligated to deliver (e.g. SLA-bound support response) fails to
  meet the contracted standard for two consecutive months, you can
  request and will receive a full refund of those two months.

---

## Transparency commitments

- **All pricing is publicly listed** at `safecadence.com/pricing`,
  including the Enterprise tier (which has a published *starting*
  price, not "Contact us").
- **Same rate for everyone at the same tier.** We don't negotiate
  pricing. A small MSP and a Fortune 500 pay the same Starter tier
  price. The only price reductions are:
  - Annual prepay discount (10% off if you pay yearly instead of
    monthly)
  - Multi-year prepay discount (15% off if you pay 2 years at once)
  - Documented design partner discount (50% off Year 1 in exchange
    for a case study)
  - Documented non-profit discount (50% off, must show 501(c)(3) or
    international equivalent)
- **No hidden enterprise pricing.** If we publish "Enterprise starts
  at $4,999/month" and you ask for Enterprise, you get billed
  $4,999/month or more (depending on scope), never less.

---

## Plan change commitments

- **30 days notice** to cancel any paid subscription. No surprise
  lock-ins; we don't auto-renew without giving you a cancellation
  window.
- **Upgrades take effect immediately** with pro-rated charge for the
  remainder of the billing cycle.
- **Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.** No refund of
  the unused portion of the higher tier, but no penalty either.
- **Cancellation is one-click** in your Stripe Customer Portal —
  not a "call us to cancel" maze.

---

## What we don't do

These are explicit non-commitments — things we won't do because they're
buyer-hostile:

- ❌ **Won't lock you in.** No multi-year contracts required.
- ❌ **Won't auto-charge for "premium support" you didn't ask for.**
  Every paid tier requires explicit opt-in.
- ❌ **Won't use dark patterns.** Cancel button is prominent. No
  countdown timers. No "are you sure?" loops.
- ❌ **Won't ransom your data.** Cancellation does not lock you out of
  your data — you can export everything via `safecadence ops export-org`
  before, during, and after cancellation.
- ❌ **Won't change the OSS license.** SafeCadence the software stays
  MIT-licensed regardless of any pricing changes to our hosted
  services. See `ROADMAP.md` for the full "free for anyone" guarantee.

## How to dispute a charge

1. Email billing@safecadence.com with the invoice number and reason.
   Response within 2 business days.
2. If unresolved, escalate via your Stripe Customer Portal "Dispute
   Charge" flow — Stripe will mediate.
3. We will not retaliate or deprioritize support for customers who
   dispute charges. That's a buyer-hostile practice and we don't do
   it.
